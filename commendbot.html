Do CS2 commendbots still work?

Most public CS2 commend bots are outdated, broken, or scams. The Steam backend changed heavily in CS2, and most “free commendbot” tools simply no longer function.

Problems with self-hosted commendbots

  • Fake downloads or malware
  • Account bans from suspicious logins
  • Dozens of accounts required
  • Maintenance, proxies & constant breakage
  • High risk for beginners
